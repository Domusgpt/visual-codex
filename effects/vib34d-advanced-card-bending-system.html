Perspective Tilt

Gentle perspective tilt with rotateX(15deg) and rotateY(10deg). Hover enhances the effect with shimmer animation and increased depth. This demonstrates the foundation of 3D card bending.
Deep Perspective
Enhanced Z-Depth
Dramatic perspective with rotateX(25deg), rotateY(-15deg), and translateZ(80px). Creates the illusion of cards floating in true 3D space. Hover pushes the card even further into dimensional depth.
Twist Warp
Multi-Axis Rotation
Complex transformation combining rotateX, rotateY, rotateZ, and skew. Creates a twisted warping effect that suggests dimensional folding. Hover intensifies all rotation axes for extreme warping.
Dimension Fold

Advanced folding effect using scaleY(0.9) and scaleX(1.15) with rotation. Simulates cards bending along multiple axes simultaneously. Hover creates extreme aspect ratio distortion.
Reality Bend

Combines rotation with translateX and translateY displacement. Creates the effect of reality bending around the card. Hover pushes the spatial distortion to its limits.
